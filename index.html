Pipe's: Flow and Resistance

Using this application, you can easily calculate the required pipe diameter when designing a heating system, taking into account all the necessary parameters. Download free from app store ↓

First stage

By entering the thermal power output and the supply and return temperatures of the coolant and coolant type, then press “Calc by power”, the application calculates the required flow volume and the recommended pipe diameter.

Second stage

In this section, you must select the type of pipes – MLC (PEX-Al-PEX), Uponor PE-Xa, Copper pipes, SteelPress pipes and Polyprorylene plastic pipe (PPR)

Next stage

Specify the required position from the list of proposed pipe sizes for the final calculation As a result, the specific losses in the pipe (Pa per m) and the flow velocity (m per sec) If the values exceed the critical ones, they will be marked in red
